SAVE OUR PENGUINS

Five of our six penguin species are in decline. Penguins and other seabirds are a treasure or taonga, and we strive to protect and conserve them and their habitat, the wider marine and coastal environment

FUTURE VISION

West Coast seabirds and their habitat are healthy and thriving.

HOW

We strive to achieve this through research, education, awareness, advocacy and practical projects, founded on strong science.
